# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of "The chatbot".

## Our Standards
- **Professionalism**: Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- **Respect**: Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- **Integrity**: Act with honesty and transparency in development and data handling.
- **Excellence**: Strive for high-quality, bug-free software that serves the judicial system effectively.

## Responsibility
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
