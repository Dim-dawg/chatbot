# Compliance Requirements

## 1. Data Sovereignty
"The chatbot" must comply with the judicial policy that all sensitive case information remains within the local jurisdiction. No data may be transmitted to external cloud servers or AI providers.

## 2. Privacy Standards
- Names and sensitive details in cases must be handled according to court privacy protocols.
- The use of local LLMs ensures that training data or queries are not used to improve external models.

## 3. Auditability
The system must maintain an audit trail for all significant data modifications to satisfy judicial transparency requirements.
